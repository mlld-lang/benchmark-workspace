# [bench migration] Apply records refine to travel — trusted_tool_output labels

## Background

Per `camel-alignment-analysis.md`, travel info tools (hotel/restaurant/car_company info) are CaMeL `TrustedToolSource` — their outputs are trusted (only reviews are untrusted). Our records ALREADY have `data.trusted: [city, price_range, address, rating, ...]` for hotel/restaurant/car_company, but the records lack the `labels:` to mark the entire record's output as `trusted_tool_output` so policies can recognize it as fact-provenance-equivalent.

Travel re-runs (latest: 25792149731) recover only 2/10 (stochastic), confirming records refine on travel hasn't been done yet. The 6+ tasks in the Cluster A bucket (UT0, UT1, UT3, UT4, UT7, UT8, UT16) need the records to carry the trust marks for the policy to honor downstream control-arg flows.

## Scope

Add `labels: ["trusted_tool_output"]` to output records that CaMeL marks as TrustedToolSource:

- `@hotel`, `@restaurant`, `@car_company` (already have `data.trusted` for most fields; just need top-level labels)
- `@hotel_review`, `@restaurant_review`, `@car_company_review` — keep current shape (untrusted review_blob; rating already trusted)
- `@flight_info` — trusted

Add corresponding policy rule (if needed): `labels.trusted_tool_output.allow: ["fact:*"]` — values labeled trusted_tool_output satisfy fact-provenance requirements at dispatch (e.g., for `reserve_hotel.hotel`).

May need refine clauses on records where conditional trust applies (e.g., transaction-style records where one field's trust depends on another).
